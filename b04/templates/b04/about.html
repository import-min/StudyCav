Welcome to Project B04!

This is your centralized app for managing study groups, events, and collaboration!

Features:

  • Create and join study groups.
  • Explore all study groups in the 'All Study Groups' page.
  • Manage and participate in events through the 'View Calendar' page.
  • View groups and any requests from other users to join on your dashboard.
  • Chat, share files, and stay stay updated on events within each group page.

Instructions:

Use the navigation bar to explore the features and manage your groups efficiently.

This project is a class project. The system is not monitored. No real infromation should be submitted.
